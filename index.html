About Me

Hi there, my name's Yash. I'm passionate about making science and technology accessible for everyone. To me, a product has a shot at success when anyone can understand why it matters. Throughout my past work in computer science, engineering, and most recently medicine, I've always worked to make sure my team never loses sight of why what we're doing is important. Let's build something great together!

What I’m Up To

Currently, I’m a student at the University of Saskatchewan, completing a B.Eng in Engineering Physics and a B.Sc in Computer Science in December 2019. I'm a founder of Soteria Medtech, a company spun out of the Summer Entrepreneurs program at the U of S, and I also work part-time at Innovation Enterprise as a Student Innovation and Business Analyst.

Aside from classes, I’m also involved with my university’s biomedical engineering group, SaskInvent, as Project Leader, along with having served as Recruitment Director and Sponsorship Director in the past. I’ve also worked in several research labs, spanning computer science, engineering, physics, and biochemistry.
