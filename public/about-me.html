About Me

What I’ve Accomplished

I recently completed my studies at the University of Saskatchewan, earning a B.Eng in Engineering Physics and a B.Sc in Computer Science in December 2019. I'm a founder of Soteria Medtech, a company spun out of the Summer Entrepreneurs program at the U of S. Previously, I worked part-time at Innovation Enterprise as a Student Innovation and Business Analyst.

During my studies, I was also heavily involved with my university’s biomedical engineering group, SaskInvent, as Project Leader, along with having served as Recruitment Director and Sponsorship Director in the past. I’ve also worked in several research labs, spanning computer science, engineering, physics, and biochemistry .

What I Do for Fun

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy traveling, theatre, reading, and a good cup of tea. I like to keep up with the innovation and entrepreneurship scene in my local area, and I’m always excited to see who thinks they have the next big idea - currently, I'm based in the Vancouver area. Meeting new people one of my favorite things, and I’m always trying to broaden my horizons.
